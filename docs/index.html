Lumi Luna Co.

Lumi Luna Co. is an encompassing brand for my artwork. The name Lumi Luna comes from the idea of a luminous moon. This is a brand I would like to expand on in the future. View my work on my website.

The Artwork I have made stems from my fascination with the moon and how its beauty influences our daily lives. I have been working in the mediums of watercolor and block printing. I plan to continue making work to add to this collecction.

My work consists of:

  • Origional paintings
  • Various size prints of work
  • Greeting cards
  • Postcards
  • Posters
